Vanilla Milkshake

Description

Here is how to make a vanilla milkshake.

Ingredients

  • sugar
  • vanilla extract
  • vanilla ice cream
  • milk
  • whipped cream
  • fruit toppings (ex: strawberries, blueberries, etc.
Equipment:
    blender
Steps:

To make vanilla milkshake:

  1. Combine sugar, vanilla extract, vanilla ice cream, and milk into the blender.
  2. Blend all the ingredients together until smooth.
  3. Serve the milkshake in a glass/cup
  4. Top with whipped cream and fruits
