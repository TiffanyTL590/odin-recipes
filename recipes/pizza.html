Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Here is a recipe on how to make pizza.

Ingredients

Pizza

  • flour
  • mozzarella cheese
  • pepperoni
  • tomato sauce/paste
  • garlic powder
  • dried, crushed basil
  • onion powder
  • sugar
  • salt
  • cooking oil
  • dry yeast
Steps

To make pizza saue:

  1. Combine tomato sauce, onion powder, garlic powder, dried & crushed basil, salt and sugar into a medium bowl
  2. Fill the bowl with 1/2 cup of water.
  3. Set aside for flavors to develop
    4. To make pizza crust:
  4. Combine 2 cups of flour with dry yeast, sugar & salt.
  5. Add water & oil and mix until blended well for 1 minute
  6. Gradually add flour slowly until a soft and sticky dough ball forms
  7. Knead the dough for 4 minutes on a floured surface until the dough is smooth and elastic
  8. Divide the dough in half & pat into greased pizza pan
  9. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees & top the crust with sauce, pepperoni & cheese.
  10. Bake the pizza for 18 to 20 minutes until the crusts are browned & cheese is melted.
